Since 2023 we've been throwing hard techno raves and alt/punk shows across Puerto Rico — and filming everything. The flyers lived on stories that disappeared in 24 hours. The ticket links lived wherever we last posted them.

Not anymore.

**mindless.pr is the permanent signal.** One link, always in the bio:

- The next event and its ticket link are always at the top of the homepage.
- The [residents](/artists) — the artists who built this with us — each have their own page.
- The [video archive](/videos) holds every aftermovie and recap.
- And this blog is where the lore lives: interviews, recaps, stories from the floor.

If you're new here: we do two things. **Dimensions** is our hard techno series — industrial BPMs, strobes, San Juan warehouse energy. And our alt/punk nights bring the island's heavier side out — live bands, mosh pits, no algorithm-friendly anything.

Follow us on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/mindlessentertainmenttv/). See you on the floor.

*— MINDLESS, San Juan PR*
